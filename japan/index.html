Events this week in China.

By: Doug Leggett

Japan.

  • THE BANK OF JAPAN ANNOUNCED AN UNSCHEDULED OPERATION OF PURCHASING THE 5-10Y BONDS.
  • LOWER HOUSE SPEAKER HIROYUKI HOSODA IS PLANNING TO STEP DOWN AT 79 YEARS OLD.
  • NEW DEFENSE CHIEF MINORU KIHARA IS SET TO VISIT WASHINGTON TO HOLD TALKS WITH U.S. DEFENSE SECRETARY LLOYD AUSTIN.
  • PLANS TO EXTEND A BULLET TRAIN ROUTE DEEPER INTO THE FUKUI PREFECTURE FROM TOKYO IS UNDERWAY.
  • MITSUBISHI MOTORS ANNOUNCED PLANS TO MOVE PRODUCTION OUT OF MAINLAND CHINA.
  • THE BOJ RAISED THE 10Y YIELD CAP TO 1% AND CONSUMER BANKS ARE CONSIDERING FLOATING MORTGAGES.
